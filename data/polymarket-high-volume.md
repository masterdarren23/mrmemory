## Volume Pattern Analysis

### Daily Volume Patterns
- **Highest activity:** During US market hours (9 AM - 5 PM ET)
- **Sports spikes:** Massive volume on game days, near-zero between events
- **Political markets:** Steady volume with spikes on news events
- **Crypto markets:** 24/7 activity but peak during US hours

### Market Lifecycle Patterns
1. **Creation:** Low volume, wide spreads
2. **Active trading:** Volume builds as event approaches
3. **Near-resolution:** Volume SPIKES as information arrives
4. **Post-resolution:** Brief high volume as positions close, then dead
