## Where Others Fail

1. **Retail traders buy YES on scary events** — they pay 47¢ for a 5-7% chance
2. **They anchor on 2024's hyperactive hurricane season** (14 hurricanes, 5 major) without noting ENSO shift
3. **They don't distinguish market criteria** — the "Natural Disaster" market requires an *extremely* specific set of outcomes
4. **They overweight recent experience** over 100+ years of base rates

## Where We Succeed

1. **We computed base rates** from 125+ years of hurricane data
2. **We factor in ENSO transition** — 2026 is La Niña → neutral/El Niño, which historically means fewer/weaker Atlantic hurricanes
3. **We exploit retail bias** in thin, neglected markets where smart money hasn't bothered
4. **We size by edge** — biggest allocation to biggest mispricing (Natural Disaster at 47% vs 5-7%)
